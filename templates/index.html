About This Tool

This AI-powered system analyzes your field conditions to recommend the most suitable crops for cultivation. The recommendation is based on machine learning models trained on agricultural data.

Optimal Conditions

  • Temperature: Most crops thrive between 15-30°C
  • Humidity: Ideal range is typically 40-80% for most crops
  • Soil pH: Neutral pH (6.0-7.5) is best for most plants
  • Water Availability: Varies by crop type

How It Works

The system uses a Random Forest machine learning algorithm trained on crop data to predict the best crop based on your input parameters.
